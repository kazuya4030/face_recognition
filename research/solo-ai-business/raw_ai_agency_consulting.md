## Analyst note on this batch
This category (AI automation/consulting agencies) has the LOWEST verifiability of any researched segment — heavy overlap with "how to start an AI agency" course-sellers who have strong incentive to inflate numbers to sell education products. Two cases (Morningside AI, Polsia) were explicitly disqualified/flagged as likely hype or no longer fitting the target profile. Treat all revenue figures in this file as self-reported unless otherwise noted, and weight this segment's real lesson as "the service/agency model is real and reachable at $10-100K/mo for a solo operator, but the widely-publicized $1M+/mo solo-agency claims are mostly marketing for courses/communities, not audited operating businesses."
