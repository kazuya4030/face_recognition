### Photo AI (+ Nomad List, Interior AI, Remote OK portfolio)
- Founder: Pieter Levels (solo)
- Country: Netherlands
- Product (what they sell): AI-generated photorealistic headshots/photoshoots (Photo AI); also runs Interior AI (AI interior redesign), Nomad List (remote-work community/database), Remote OK (job board)
- Customers (who buys): Consumers wanting AI photos; remote workers/digital nomads; remote job seekers/employers
- Revenue/ARR: Photo AI ~$132-138K MRR (Nov 2025) ≈ $1.6M ARR; Interior AI ~$40K/mo; Remote OK ~$35-41K/mo; Nomad List ~$38K/mo (2025 figures cited at $5.3M ARR by one aggregator). Combined portfolio reportedly $2.5-3M+/year. Method: founder's own public revenue dashboards + build-in-public tweets, aggregated by multiple outlets (Indie Hackers deep-dive, ppc.land, Latka). Confidence: press-reported/self-reported (founder discloses live revenue publicly, widely corroborated but exact figures vary by source)
- Profit margin: not precisely disclosed; widely described as very high margin (minimal team, self-hosted infra)
- Employees: ~2 (down from 10 in 2023); described as "zero employees" in some periods
- AI usage: AI (Stable Diffusion-based models) IS the product itself (image generation); Levels also codes the apps himself (PHP), not built via AI coding assistants
- Customer acquisition channel: 10+ years of X/Twitter build-in-public audience (~600K followers), SEO
- Source URL(s): https://www.indiehackers.com/post/photo-ai-by-pieter-levels-complete-deep-dive-case-study-0-to-132k-mrr-in-18-months-3a9a2b1579 ; https://ppc.land/how-one-photo-ai-app-generates-132k-monthly-after-70-failed-startups/ ; https://getlatka.com/companies/nomad-list
