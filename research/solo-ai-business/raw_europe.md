### lemlist
- Founder: Guillaume Moubeche, Vianney Lecroart, François Lecroart (3-person founding team)
- Country: France (Paris)
- Product: AI-personalized cold email and multichannel (email/LinkedIn/WhatsApp/phone) outreach platform
- Customers: B2B sales teams, SDRs, agencies, founders doing outbound
- Revenue/ARR: Crossed $1M ARR in early 2020 (from $600K ARR Dec 2019) as a 3-person team; now $40M+ ARR (2024) with 37,000+ customers — cited for the historical $1M-ARR-at-3-people milestone. Press-reported (founder interviews, company blog)
- Profit margin: not disclosed
- Employees: 3 at $1M ARR milestone; scaled much larger since
- AI usage: AI-personalized email copywriting/subject lines, large lead database, AI-assisted deliverability tooling
- Customer acquisition channel: Content marketing/SEO, build-in-public, community and affiliate programs
- Source URL(s): https://www.stacksync.com/blog/one-thousand-dollars-in-a-paris-flat-the-origin-story-of-lemlist ; https://www.founderclub.com/guillame-moubeche-lemlist-bootstrapped-from-0-to-8m-arr-with-0-vc-monies-in-5-years/

### Outrank
- Founder: Thibault "Tibo" Louis-Lucas
- Country: France (Paris)
- Product: AI-powered SEO content automation (auto-generates and publishes SEO blog content)
- Customers: Bloggers, SaaS marketing teams, SEO agencies (2,500+ sites using it)
- Revenue/ARR: $300K+ MRR (~$3.6M+ ARR) as of April 2026, self-reported directly via running X MRR updates (from $20K MRR start-2025 to $274K by April 2026 to $300K+ shortly after)
- Employees: Part of TMAKER studio (8-10 total across products — exceeds strict 0-3 target, flagged)
- AI usage: AI-generated SEO-optimized articles, automated keyword research and auto-publishing
- Customer acquisition channel: Affiliate program (~$10K/mo paid out), X build-in-public, cross-promotion
- Source URL(s): https://x.com/tibo_maker/status/2044709929416573374 ; https://www.outrank.so/

### Daymaker
- Founder: William Lindholm (+ two colleagues, team of 3)
- Country: Norway
- Product: Sales/HR outreach automation sending personalized cakes instead of cold emails/birthday gifts ("cold caking"); built entirely on AI app-builder Lovable with no hand-written code
- Customers: B2B sales teams doing outbound; HR/People teams automating employee gifting
- Revenue/ARR: Conflicting figures: ~$110-130K/month (~$1.3-1.5M annualized) per a tweet from Lovable's CEO, vs. Dealroom.co's "$150K ARR in five months" — self-reported-unverified, early-stage, unreconciled
- Employees: 3 at launch
- AI usage: Entire product (HR integrations, accounting, logistics) built via AI no-code platform Lovable — a notable case of a NON-technical team building and operating a business purely through AI tooling, directly relevant to the reader's non-engineer profile
- Customer acquisition channel: Viral press coverage (Forbes, Inc.), direct outbound using its own "cold caking" method
- Source URL(s): https://www.forbes.com/sites/sandycarter/2026/04/04/openai-called-the-one-person-ai-startup-and-three-founders--proved-it/ ; https://www.inc.com/mariapaula-gonzalez/this-founder-used-ai-to-build-a-thriving-business-out-of-customized-cake-pitches/91325967 ; https://app.dealroom.co/news/note/daymaker-reaches-150k-arr-in-five-months-by-turning-cake-deliveries-into-an-automated-hr-tool
