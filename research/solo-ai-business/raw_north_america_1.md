# Raw research batch: North America solo AI SaaS/agency cases (unedited agent output; some overlap with other batches — dedup happens in final CaseStudies.md)

### Cal AI
- Founder: Zach Yadegari (with co-founder Henry Langmack)
- Country: United States (Roslyn, New York)
- Product: Mobile app estimating calories/macros from a food photo (AI computer vision + nutrition DB)
- Customers: Consumers tracking diet/weight loss
- Revenue/ARR: ~$28K first month, $115K second month (2024); ~$30M revenue in 2025; $5.7M in Jan 2026 alone (~$50M annualized run rate); ~$1.4M/month gross profit after app-store cuts — press-reported (CNBC, Inc., Entrepreneur), founder-disclosed but corroborated across outlets
- Profit margin: High (~$1.4M/month gross profit cited directly)
- Employees: 2 teen co-founders at core; small supporting team as it scaled (not fully disclosed)
- AI usage: Core product is AI/computer-vision food recognition + calorie estimation from photos
- Customer acquisition channel: Viral TikTok, App Store organic + paid UA
- Source URL(s): https://www.cnbc.com/2025/09/06/cal-ai-how-a-teenage-ceo-built-a-fast-growing-calorie-tracking-app.html ; https://www.inc.com/ben-sherry/he-built-an-ai-app-in-high-school-made-40m-and-sold-to-myfitnesspal-now-hes-aiming-even-bigger/91307748

### Rezi
- Founder: Jacob Jacquet
- Country: United States (Palo Alto, CA)
- Product: AI-powered resume builder (GPT-based resume writing/ATS optimization)
- Customers: Job seekers, 2.6M-4M+ cumulative users
- Revenue/ARR: Figures inconsistent across sources: "$5M+ lifetime revenue," "$2.5M ARR," "$215K MRR" — self-reported-unverified, treat as directional
- Profit margin: not disclosed precisely
- Employees: Started solo 2019; current team small/lean, not clearly disclosed
- AI usage: First resume builder to integrate GPT (GPT-3, Dec 2020, pre-ChatGPT)
- Customer acquisition channel: Free template gone viral on Reddit, then SEO/organic
- Source URL(s): https://launchpedia.co/rezi-growth-strategy/ ; https://www.wearefounders.uk/from-resume-template-to-5m-saas-how-jacob-built-rezi/

### Bannerbear
- Founder: Jon Yongfook
- Country: UK national, based in Singapore
- Product: API/no-code tool auto-generating marketing images/video at scale from templates + data feeds
- Customers: Marketing teams, developers, agencies needing programmatic visual generation
- Revenue/ARR: $1M ARR (Sept 2025), grew from ~$630K ARR (2023-24), ~$991K (2024); ~5-6 years to reach $1M — self-reported, unusually transparent/consistent reporter
- Profit margin: not precisely disclosed; lean, sustainable one-person SaaS
- Employees: 0 (solo)
- AI usage: AI/automation image & video generation pipelines (template + data -> generated visual assets)
- Customer acquisition channel: Content/SEO, developer community, deliberately non-viral slow organic growth
- Source URL(s): https://arrfounder.com/news/bannerbear-reaches-1m-arr ; https://www.bannerbear.com/journey-to-10k-mrr/ ; https://medium.com/superframeworks/bannerbear-630k-arr-simple-api-to-generate-images-23ff54509f99

### Polsia
- Founder: Ben Broca (aka Ben Cera)
- Country: United States (San Francisco, relocated from France)
- Product: "AI CEO agent" platform autonomously building/operating many small automated companies
- Revenue/ARR: $1M ARR within ~30 days of launch (late 2025); ~$6.2M ARR by April 2026; $10M run rate by May 2026, then raised $30M (Sound Ventures, True Ventures) at $250M valuation — press-reported (Fortune) + self-reported (LinkedIn). CAUTION: extraordinary/unauditable claims, now VC-funded so no longer bootstrapped by 2026. Cross-referenced with the other batch's identical flagging of this company as hype-adjacent.
- Employees: 0 claimed at $1M-$10M ARR run (notable outlier)
- AI usage: AI agents are the entire operating structure
- Source URL(s): https://fortune.com/2026/03/26/the-one-person-unicorn-myth-miracle-future-of-startups-polsia/ ; https://app.dealroom.co/news/note/polsia-hits-10m-arr-with-zero-employees-raises-30m

### Aragon AI
- Founder: Wesley Tian (co-founded with Akhil N.)
- Country: United States (San Francisco)
- Product: AI headshot/photo generation suite (HeadshotPro competitor)
- Revenue/ARR: $10M revenue as of April 2025, profitable, self-funded, no outside VC — self-reported via founder podcast interviews, corroborated across multiple sources
- Employees: Started 2 co-founders, grew to 13 within 12 months, stayed ~10-11 — EXCEEDS 0-3 constraint from early on; included as contrast/benchmark only
- AI usage: Fine-tuned generative image models producing headshots
- Source URL(s): https://www.indiehackers.com/post/tech/building-one-of-the-first-ai-headshot-products-and-hitting-900k-mo-in-3-years-UK9omiPofFtha5Kps2Fj ; https://getlatka.com/companies/aragon.ai

### Formula Bot (Excel Formula Bot)
- Founder: David Bressler
- Country: United States (Orlando, FL)
- Product: AI natural-language-to-Excel-formula tool, expanded to "AI data analyst"
- Customers: 1M+ users (~650K freemium, ~10,900 paid)
- Revenue/ARR: ~$24,000/month revenue (~$21,000/month profit), i.e. roughly $250-290K/year — does NOT clear $1M bar; included solely for very high disclosed profit margin (~87.5%)
- Employees: Founder describes as "one-person company" but pays 2 part-time Bubble no-code devs ~20 hrs/week combined
- AI usage: LLM-based natural-language-to-formula/data-analysis engine
- Customer acquisition channel: Built on Bubble, SEO/content for "Excel formula generator" queries
- Source URL(s): https://www.starterstory.com/stories/excelformulabot ; https://www.indiehackers.com/post/case-study-excel-ai-bot-makes-projected-164-850-profit-in-12-months-402bf5338c
