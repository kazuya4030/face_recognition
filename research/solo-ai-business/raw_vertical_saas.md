## Key finding for construction/recruiting relevance
The two most figure-verifiable examples closest to the reader's exact interest area (construction AI: Togal.AI; recruiting AI: Talenter AI) BOTH already exceed the 0-3 person target once they reach meaningful revenue ($2M+ and $440K respectively) — reinforcing a pattern seen across the whole research project: vertical B2B AI SaaS in operationally complex industries (construction, insurance, legal, recruiting) tends to require a slightly larger team (3-30 people) than consumer-AI or creator-tool categories to handle vertical-specific sales cycles, compliance, and integration work. Fathom AI Technologies (medical aesthetics, 3 people, 90%+ margin, $300K ARR in 12 weeks) is the closest verified example of an AI-agent-run micro-team in a complex B2B vertical — a strong reference case for a construction/HR vertical AI SaaS built by a very small team.
